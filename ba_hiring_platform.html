Role Details — What You'll Own

Counselling Team

+

You'll be the analytical backbone for the counselling business — owning the dashboards leadership depends on and finding signal in a noisy sales funnel.

Own and manage central Power BI dashboards tracking key business metrics across centres. Analyse end-to-end sales funnels — lead generation, qualification, conversions to registrations and admissions, along with retention and performance metrics to drive revenue growth.

Deliver actionable insights through weekly/monthly business reviews with leadership. Write complex SQL queries and handle large datasets. Perform data cleaning, transformation, and automation using Python. Monitor KPIs, identify trends and gaps, and proactively recommend interventions.

Funnel AnalyticsPower BI DashboardsSQL ReportingPython AutomationBusiness ReviewsKPI Monitoring

Category Marketing Team

+

The core of the job is being the analytical layer between media buying and business outcomes — translating raw platform data (Google Ads, Meta, etc.) into decisions about where money should go.

You can spot whether a performance issue is a data problem or a campaign structure problem. Your analysis directly informs budget allocation, creative strategy, and channel mix.

Campaign Performance AnalysisBudgeting & Spend AnalysisReporting & DashboardingA/B Testing & ExperimentationAudience & Attribution AnalysisCross-functional Collaboration

Operations Team

+

You'll track operational efficiency — from SLA compliance and TAT analysis to process bottlenecks. Your analysis will directly feed decisions on ground operations and process improvement.

Ops KPI TrackingSLA & TAT AnalysisProcess EfficiencyOps Dashboards

Academics Team

+

You'll support the central academic ops function — tracking NPS across cohorts, running test performance analysis, supporting talent nurture initiatives, and owning central academic ops tasks. At-risk identification is part of the work but a smaller slice of a broader mandate.

NPS Tracking & AnalysisTest Performance AnalysisTalent Nurture SupportCentral Acad Ops TasksOutcome ReportingAttendance & EngagementAt-Risk Flagging
